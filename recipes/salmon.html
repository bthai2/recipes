Pan-Seared Salmon

Description

Learn how long to cook salmon on the stove with this easy pan-seared salmon recipe. The salmon fillets are simply seasoned with salt, pepper, and capers. Garnish with slices of lemon.

Ingredients

  • 4 (6 ounce) fillets salmon
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons capers
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 4 slices lemon

Steps

  1. Coat salmon fillets with olive oil
  2. Place skin-side down in the preheated skillet and increase heat to high.
  3. Sprinkle with capers, salt, and pepper
  4. Cook for 3 minutes on one side.
  5. Turn salmon fillets over
  6. Continue to cook until salmon flakes easily with a fork, about 5 minutes.
  7. Transfer salmon to individual plates and garnish with lemon slices.
